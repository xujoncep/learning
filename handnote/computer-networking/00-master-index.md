## 📚 Course Overview

এই course-এ মোট **8টা chapter** আছে — fundamentals থেকে advanced topics পর্যন্ত। প্রতিটা chapter-এ concept + code examples + MCQ + written problems + traps + summary।

## 📖 Chapters

| # | Chapter | What you'll learn |
|---|---------|-------------------|
| 01 | [Fundamentals](01-fundamentals.md) | Network কী ও কেন, devices, transmission media, topologies, transmission modes। |
| 02 | [OSI & TCP/IP Models](02-osi-tcp-ip-models.md) | OSI 7-layer, TCP/IP 4-layer, encapsulation/decapsulation। |

| 08 | [Advanced & Practical](08-advanced-practical.md) | Wireless, cloud, troubleshooting commands, design, emerging tech। |

4. **Concept gap?** — সংশ্লিষ্ট chapter-এর "Concept" + "Code Examples" দেখো।
